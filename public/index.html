01
the mechanism

Three moves, then it runs itself.

Pick the supply

Paste your token CA and choose how much supply to drop. You send it to the public pool wallet, where it's held and never spent on anything else.

Hit the cap

You set a target market cap. Nothing happens until the token reaches it on-chain. The trigger is the cap, not a date or a promise.

Snapshot + drop

At the cap, holders #1–#200 (minus the LP) are snapshotted and split an even cut. At every ×2 after, a fresh snapshot and the next half of what's left.

02
the ladder

Every ×2 is a new round.

The cap climbs in doublings. Each rung fires a new round that snapshots holders again and drops the next portion of the supply. Default is 50% of the remaining balance per round, so payouts taper as the cap grows.

03
the holders

Who actually gets the drop.

Every holder from #1 to #200 is in. The only wallet skipped is the liquidity pool, because dropping tokens back into the LP isn't a reward to anyone.

The cut is split evenly, so a rank-1 wallet and a rank-200 wallet receive the same. No weighting, no games.

The range is configurable per order in advanced settings, but the default #1 to #200 is the platform norm.

faq

Straight answers.

How does a deposit reach my order?

You send the supply to one public pool wallet and include the memo code we give you. The bot matches the incoming transfer by that memo and your token's mint, then marks the order funded.

Which holders are included in a snapshot?

Every holder ranked #1 to #200 at snapshot time. The only wallet excluded is the liquidity pool, so the drop never just recycles tokens back into the LP. The range is adjustable per order in advanced settings.

What does "every ×2" mean exactly?

After the first snapshot at your target cap, a new round fires each time the market cap doubles. Each round takes a fresh holder snapshot and drops the next portion (50% of the remaining balance by default).

Is anything custodial?

The supply sits in the public pool wallet until its rounds fire. Every order and its rounds are visible on the live pool, so nothing happens off the books.

Do I need to connect a wallet?

No. You create the order with just the CA and send the supply manually with the memo. No signing on the site.
